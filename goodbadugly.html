The Good, the Bad
and the Ugly

Synopsis

In the Southwest during the Civil War, a mysterious stranger, Joe (Clint Eastwood), and a Mexican outlaw, Tuco (Eli Wallach), form an uneasy alliance. Joe turns in the bandit for the reward money, then rescues him just as he is being hanged. When Joe's scheme starts to backfire due to Tuco's greed, the pair are forced to hunt for buried Confederate gold. They are joined by Angel Eyes (Lee Van Cleef), a sadistic mercenary who will stop at nothing to get his hands on the fortune.

Cast & Crew

Director: Sergio Leone

Cast: Clint Eastwood, Eli Wallach, Lee Van Cleef, Aldo Giuffrè, Luigi Pistilli, Rada Rassimov
