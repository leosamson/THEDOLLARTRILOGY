For a Few Dollars More

Synopsis

Two bounty hunters with completely different intentions team up to track down a Western outlaw. Monco is a bounty killer, a loner that operates on his own. Col. Douglas Mortimer is an older, more experienced bounty killer that always carries a Bible with him. They team up to hunt down El Indio, a ruthless, psychopathic bank robber and killer.

Cast & Crew

Director: Sergio Leone

Cast: Clint Eastwood, Lee Van Cleef, Gian Maria Volontè, Mara Krupp, Luigi Pistilli, Klaus Kinski
